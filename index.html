Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Chico drivers want to know about ceramic coating, paint correction, and our detail services.

How long does a System X ceramic coating last?

System X ceramic coating is a revolutionary advance in paint protection — an exclusive, permanent ceramic coating that protects exterior surfaces for the lifetime of new cars and up to 8 years on used cars when properly maintained.

Do I need paint correction before ceramic coating?

In most cases, yes. Ceramic coating locks in the condition of the paint at the time it's applied — including swirls or scratches. We always assess your paint and recommend the correction level needed to give you the finish you actually want.

Do you offer auto repair or paint work?

No. Full Attack Motorsports is a detailing and protection shop only — we are not an auto repair shop and not an auto paint shop. We are, however, located right next door to Chico Car Care and can coordinate repairs with them if needed.

Do you detail boats and RVs?

Yes. Boats and RVs take serious weather abuse and clean up beautifully after a careful two-day detail. We bring the same precision to your boat or RV that we do to a daily driver or weekend build.

How are prices determined?

Listed prices are an estimate for a vehicle of average size and condition. Actual price depends on the size, type, and condition of the vehicle. The quote you receive after your consultation is the price you pay.
